Subscriptions

  • Restore purchases: Open the app → Premium screen → tap Restore purchases.
  • Cancel subscription: iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Mon Bon Voyage → Cancel.
  • Billing: Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before renewal.

FAQ

  • I can’t restore purchases. Make sure you’re signed into the same Apple ID used to purchase.
  • The app isn’t saving. Try closing and reopening the app, then update to the latest version.
